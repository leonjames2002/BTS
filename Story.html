The Story of BTS

Beyond the Scene: The Global K-Pop Sensation

Timeline

  • 2010: BTS is formed by Big Hit Entertainment.
  • June 2013: BTS debuts with the single album 2 Cool 4 Skool.
  • 2015: Gains international attention with The Most Beautiful Moment in Life series.
  • 2017: Wins Top Social Artist at the Billboard Music Awards, the first K-pop group to do so.
  • 2018: Speaks at the United Nations, launching the "Love Myself" campaign with UNICEF.
  • 2020: Releases Dynamite, their first all-English single, topping the Billboard Hot 100.
  • 2021: Nominated for a Grammy Award and continues to break global records.
  • Present: BTS remains one of the most influential music groups worldwide, inspiring millions of fans (ARMY) with their music and message.

Debut

BTS officially debuted on June 13, 2013, under Big Hit Entertainment with the single album 2 Cool 4 Skool. Their debut stage was on
M! Countdown.

Other Activities

  • United Nations speeches (2018, 2020, 2021)
  • Web series: Run BTS!, Bon Voyage
  • Documentaries: Burn the Stage, Break the Silence
  • Global campaigns: Love Myself with UNICEF
  • Featured in TIME's 100 Most Influential People (2019, 2020)
  • Multiple Billboard Music Awards, American Music Awards, and Grammy nominations

Hiatus & Solo Activities

In June 2022, BTS announced a group hiatus to focus on solo projects and military service. Members have since released solo albums and continued various individual activities.

Future Plans

As of October 2023, BTS is expected to regroup in 2025 after completing military service. Fans eagerly await their return and new music.
