Bangtan Sonyeondan

Leaders of K-Pop

Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단) means “Bulletproof Boy Scouts in Korean.It symbolizes their mission to protect youth from societal pressures and expectations.”

It has Seven Members named RM,Jin,Suga,Jhope,Jimin,V and Jungkook.Each members are strongly well known because of they own ablities and talents.

No More Dreams (their debut song) (2013) the first apperance of them made a history. Run (2015), Fake love (20181), DNA (2017),are the best songs of theirs for all time.”

The Albums in 2020Map ot the soul 7,journey (2020), Map ot the soul 7 (2020), and BE (2020),their lyrics and meaningful songs spread throughout the world and reach to the hearts of fans. Not only because of their music, also the gentle hearts of theirs can save a lot of teenage life.

Latest Album as Group

In 2022,Proof(Album name) released as the last group project before they enlisted to the military.

There are 48 tracks(3 verson of CD) in that album including their old songs and new unrelased songs. In CD 1:19 tracks featuring BTS's major singles from their debut in 2013 up to 2021, along with the new song "Yet to Come (The Most Beautiful Moment)." In CD 2: 15 tracks selected by the members, including solo and sub-unit songs, plus the new track "Run BTS." In CD 3: 14 tracks, primarily demos and unreleased songs. Of these, only "For Youth" is available in the digital edition; the remaining 13 tracks are exclusive to the physical CDs.
